Analyzing the Housing Market in Metro Manila: Insights from Power BI

Understanding housing market insights in the capital region and largest metropolitan area of the Philippines

Introduction

Understanding housing trends is essential for buyers and investors. This article presents an analysis of the housing market in Metro Manila, utilizing a dataset sourced from the Lamudi platform. By employing Power BI for visualization and Python for data cleaning, I take the challenge of revealing key insights into property listings and market trends.

Data Overview

The dataset from Kaggle comprises records of real estate listings with the following columns:

  • House ID: Unique listing identifier.
  • Description: Details about the property.
  • Location: Address. Contains city and region.
  • Price: Listing price.
  • Floor Area: Area in square meters.
  • Land Area: Land area in square meters.

Data cleaning was conducted using Power Query and Python, encompassing several key steps. First, city names were extracted from the Location column to enable city/municipality identification. Next, a mapping was created to filter the dataset for listings specifically within Metro Manila. Additionally, properties were categorized into housing types and storey levels by identifying relevant keywords in the Description column. Finally, to address the 3% of missing values in the Price column, median prices by city were calculated and imputed using Python, ensuring a more robust dataset for analysis.

KPIs

To provide a concise overview, I included four critical KPIs in the dashboard:

  1. Median Price: Average prices by city and type.
  2. Total Listings: Count of listings per city.
  3. Average Price per Square Meter: Insight into price density.
  4. Land Area to Price Ratio: Evaluating land area pricing.

Dashboard

KPI section shows values we want to know depending on our selection on the dashboard. The filter pane adjusts the independent variables - city, house type, and storey level. The parameter section lets us choose a metric either Median Price in millions of Pesos, Price per square meter in thousands of Pesos, or Total Listings.

Insights

Median Prices

Based on property listings from Lamudi, the median house price in Metro Manila was Php 28.65 million, with a median price of Php 212,580 per square meter. Makati City, known for its central business district, stood out with a staggering median price of Php 152 million and an average of Php 643,600 per square meter. Taguig, home to the upscale Bonifacio Global City, followed closely with a median price of Php 112 million and Php 280,500 per square meter. Other cities, such as Marikina (Php 9.7M), Valenzuela (Php 7.4M), and Caloocan (Php 4.5M), offered the most inexpensive options.
